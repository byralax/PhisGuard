PhishGuard Privacy Policy

At PhishGuard, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how our browser extension collects, uses, and protects your information as you use our service to protect yourself from phishing attacks.

Summary: PhishGuard is designed to protect you from phishing websites while respecting your privacy. We collect minimal data necessary to provide this service, never sell your personal information, and give you control over what data is collected.

Information We Collect

URLs You Visit

PhishGuard checks the URLs of websites you visit against databases of known phishing sites. This is necessary to provide our core service of protecting you from phishing attacks.

  • We do not store a history of the websites you visit on our servers
  • URL checking is performed locally or through secure API calls to phishing databases
  • Only suspicious URLs that are confirmed as phishing sites may be reported to improve our detection systems

Website Content

When you visit a website, PhishGuard may analyze the content of the page to detect phishing indicators such as:

  • Suspicious forms requesting sensitive information
  • Brand impersonation attempts
  • Hidden elements that might be capturing data

This analysis is performed locally in your browser. We do not store or transmit the content of websites you visit.

Usage Statistics

If enabled in settings (on by default), we collect anonymous usage statistics to help improve the extension:

  • Number of websites checked
  • Number of phishing attempts blocked
  • Types of phishing attacks detected

This data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify individual users or browsing habits.

User-Provided Information

You may optionally provide information when:

  • Reporting a suspected phishing site (including your email if you choose to provide it)
  • Submitting feedback about the extension

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Protect you from phishing attacks by identifying malicious websites
  • Improve our phishing detection algorithms
  • Fix bugs and enhance the functionality of the extension
  • Develop new features based on user needs
  • Respond to your reports and feedback

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information with third parties. We may share limited information in the following circumstances:

Third-Party Phishing Databases

PhishGuard uses third-party services like PhishTank and Google Safe Browsing to check URLs. When you visit a website, the URL may be sent to these services to check if it's a known phishing site. These services have their own privacy policies:

  • PhishTank Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

Reported Phishing Sites

When you report a phishing site through the extension, the URL and your description of the site (but not your personal information) may be shared with:

  • Our phishing database
  • Partner anti-phishing organizations
  • Relevant authorities in cases of serious fraud
Note: If you provide your email address when reporting a site, it will only be used to contact you for additional information if needed and will not be shared with third parties.

Your Choices and Controls

PhishGuard gives you control over your data through the extension's settings page:

  • Enable/disable URL checking against different phishing databases
  • Enable/disable content scanning
  • Enable/disable anonymous usage statistics
  • Enable/disable automatic reporting of detected phishing sites

You can access these settings by clicking on the PhishGuard icon in your browser toolbar and selecting "Settings".

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • All communication with our servers and third-party APIs uses secure HTTPS connections
  • API keys are stored securely in your browser's local storage
  • We regularly review our security practices to ensure your data remains protected

Children's Privacy

PhishGuard is not directed at individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us to have it removed.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes through the extension or our website.
